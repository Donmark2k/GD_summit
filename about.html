GRAPHIC DESIGNERS GLOBAL SUMMIT 2023

The Global Summit brings together the community of experts, academics, activists who comprise the Graphic designs
affiliate network in a different country every two years.
This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared
projects that advance the cause of the graphics, free culture and open knowledge.
So if you are active and engaged in the worlds of open control and knowledge-free software advocates, Wikipedia, Open Knowledge, galleries, libraries, museums, archives, governments and foundations, lawyers, and activists-we hope, you will consider joining us to build a stronger, more vibrants commons together.

Please contact us per Email for any further questions about GD Global Summit 2023!
